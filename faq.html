Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this app free to use?
Basic features are free, but some premium content may require a purchase.
How is news collected?
News is gathered automatically using AI and updated frequently.
How accurate is AI summarization?
AI-generated summaries are for reference only and may not always be fully accurate.
Do you offer support?
We do not offer individual support. Most common questions are answered in this FAQ.
What if the app is not working?
Check for updates or restart the app.
Where can I check the Terms & Privacy Policy?
You can find them in the Settings screen or on our official website.
How can I opt out of cookies?
You can adjust your preferences via the cookie consent banner or your browser settings.
How do you handle analytics and tracking?
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. No personally identifiable information is tracked or stored.
How can I opt out of data tracking?
You can opt out by submitting a request via our Google Form, linked from the Privacy Policy and in-app FAQ section.
