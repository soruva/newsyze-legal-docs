Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the app free to use?
Yes. You can read up to 5 articles for free each day.
Premium unlocks:
  • Unlimited articles
  • AI translations (5 languages)
  • Advanced Text-to-Speech
  • Full Word Bank features
  • Ad-free experience
Where does the news come from?
News articles are provided through the GNews API, which aggregates headlines from worldwide publishers.
Images are retrieved using Safe Pexels Filtering to avoid unsafe content.
How accurate are the AI summaries?
Summaries are generated using trusted AI models and are designed for learning and educational purposes only.
They simplify content and may not always match the full original article.
What is the Word Bank?
Long-press any word to translate it instantly and save it to your personal Word Bank.
Saved words are stored only on your device and can be marked as “mastered.”
Do you collect personal data?
No. Newsyze does not require login and does not collect personal information.
All preferences (languages, streak, saved words) remain only on your device.
Does the app support Text-to-Speech?
Yes. TTS supports English, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese.
You can adjust speed, pitch, and enable high-quality voices if your device supports them.
The app is not working. What should I do?
Try the following steps:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update Newsyze to the latest version
  • Restart the app
  • Reinstall if issues continue
