12. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

Most web browsers and some mobile apps include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") setting to signal that you do not wish to have your online activities tracked. However, there is no consistent industry standard for recognizing DNT signals. Therefore, we currently do not respond to DNT signals or similar mechanisms.

Under California law, we must inform you that, due to the lack of a standard, we do not respond to DNT browser signals at this time. If a standard is adopted in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.
