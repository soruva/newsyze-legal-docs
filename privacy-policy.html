1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

In Short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

All payment data is processed and stored by Google Play. You can view their privacy policy here: Google Privacy Policy.

Application Data

If you use our application(s), we may collect the following information if you grant us access or permission:

  • Mobile Device Data: Device ID, model, manufacturer, OS, system configuration, IP address, carrier, etc.

This data is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our application(s), assist with troubleshooting, and perform internal analytics and reporting.

All personal information you provide to us must be accurate, complete, and up to date, and you must notify us of any changes.

Information Automatically Collected

In Short: Some information — such as your IP address and device characteristics — is automatically collected when you use our Services.

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate our Services. This data does not reveal your specific identity (such as your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information.

Like many companies, we also collect information via cookies and similar technologies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

  • Log and Usage Data: Service-related, diagnostic, usage, and performance data.
  • Device Data: Device type, OS, IP address, hardware model, etc.

Google API: Our use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. WHAT LEGAL BASES DO WE RELY ON TO PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We only process your personal information when we have a lawful basis under applicable regulations (e.g., GDPR, UK GDPR, PIPEDA).

If you are located in the EU or UK:

We may rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: When you give us explicit permission for specific processing. You may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Performance of a Contract: When processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you.
  • Legitimate Interests: When processing is necessary for our business operations and does not override your rights (e.g., improving services, preventing fraud).
  • Legal Obligations: When processing is required to comply with laws or respond to legal processes.
  • Vital Interests: When necessary to protect life or safety.

If you are located in Canada:

We may process your information based on express or implied consent, depending on the situation.

In certain cases, we may process your information without consent if required or permitted by law, such as:

  • Emergencies where obtaining consent is not feasible.
  • Investigations related to fraud or breaches of law.
  • Compliance with subpoenas, warrants, or court orders.
  • Processing for journalistic, artistic, or research purposes.
  • Where information is publicly available as permitted by regulations.
