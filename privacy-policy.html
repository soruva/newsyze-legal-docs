1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

In Short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

We collect personal information when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, when you participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise when you contact us.

Sensitive Information: We do not process sensitive information.

Payment Data: We may collect data necessary to process your payments if you choose to make purchases, such as your payment instrument number and the security code associated with your payment method. All payment data is processed and stored by Google Play. You can view their privacy policy here: Google Privacy Policy.

Application Data

If you use our application(s), we may collect the following information if you grant us access or permission:

  • Mobile Device Data: Device ID, model, manufacturer, OS, system configuration, IP address, carrier, etc.
  • Push Notifications: We may request to send you push notifications. You can opt out via your device settings.

This data is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our application(s), assist with troubleshooting, and perform internal analytics and reporting.

All personal information you provide to us must be accurate, complete, and up to date, and you must notify us of any changes.

Information Automatically Collected

In Short: Some information — such as your IP address and device characteristics — is automatically collected when you use our Services.

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate our Services. This data does not reveal your specific identity (such as your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information.

Like many companies, we also collect information via cookies and similar technologies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

  • Log and Usage Data: Service-related, diagnostic, usage, and performance data.
  • Device Data: Device type, OS, IP address, hardware model, etc.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location depending on your device settings. Opt-out is available.

Google API: Our use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
