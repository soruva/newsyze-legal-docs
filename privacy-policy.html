PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: November 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how SoruvaLab (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application Newsyze and related services (“Services”).

If you have questions, contact us at: soruvalab@gmail.com

SUMMARY

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • All personal data remains on your device (local storage only).
  • No advertising, tracking, or third-party behavioral profiling.
  • AI features use temporary, anonymous processing only.
  • Payments and subscriptions are handled securely by Google Play.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information You Provide:
Only if you contact us directly (email or feedback form). We do not collect names, addresses, login details, phone numbers, or contacts.

Payment Data:
All purchases and subscriptions are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not access or store credit card, payment method, or purchase details.

Automatically Collected Data:
Newsyze does not use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Advertising ID, or any tracking tools. However, Google Play Console may provide anonymized device and performance information such as:

  • App version
  • Device type & OS version
  • Crash logs (anonymous)
  • Installation and uninstallation statistics

These are aggregated reports provided by Google and are not personally identifiable to us.

AI Features:
Text submitted for summarization, translation, or flashcard generation is:

  • processed temporarily,
  • not stored,
  • not used for model training,
  • not linked to any user.

AI usage is strictly for educational purposes (vocabulary learning, comprehension, and reading aid).

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

  • To operate and improve core features (summaries, translations, TTS, flashcards)
  • To ensure stability and detect performance issues
  • To analyze aggregated usage trends (non-identifiable)
  • To comply with legal, safety, and platform requirements

3. LEGAL BASES (EU/UK)

We process data only under lawful bases such as consent, legitimate interest, contract necessity, or legal obligation under GDPR/UK GDPR.

4. SHARING INFORMATION

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Limited sharing only when required:

  • Google Play — subscription handling, purchase validation
  • AI API providers — temporary anonymous text processing only

No advertising networks, SDKs, or data brokers are connected to Newsyze.

5. AI FEATURES & RESPONSIBLE USE

Newsyze provides AI-powered summaries, translations (5 languages), vocabulary learning, and text-to-speech. All AI output is educational support—not factual, legal, medical, or professional advice.

AI processing:

  • does not identify users
  • does not store prompts
  • does not train any model
  • is deleted after the request is completed

6. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

AI processing may occur in the United States or other regions depending on infrastructure. For EU/UK/Swiss users, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards apply.
