PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: Oct 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how SoruvaLab ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application Newsyze and related services ("Services").

If you have questions, please contact us at support@soruvalab.com.

SUMMARY

  • No account or sign-in is required to use Newsyze.
  • All personal data remains on your device (local storage).
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking is implemented.
  • AI features use anonymous text processing only.
  • Payments are processed securely via Google Play.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information You Provide: Only if you contact us directly (e.g., by email or feedback form). We do not collect profile data, contacts, or login details.

Payment Data: All payments and subscriptions are handled by Google Play. We do not have access to or store your card information.

Automatically Collected Data: Device type, OS version, app version, crash reports, and general usage statistics (via Firebase or Google Play Console).

AI Features: Text you submit for summarization, translation, or flashcard generation is processed temporarily and not stored or used for training.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

  • To operate and improve Newsyze’s core features (summaries, translations, flashcards, TTS)
  • To ensure app stability and diagnose performance issues
  • To analyze aggregated usage trends (non-identifiable)
  • To comply with applicable law and app store policies

3. LEGAL BASES (EU/UK)

We process data only under lawful bases such as consent, legitimate interest, contract necessity, or legal obligation under GDPR/UK GDPR.

4. SHARING INFORMATION

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data. Limited sharing occurs only as necessary for app functionality:

  • Google Play (for subscriptions and purchase validation)
  • Firebase (for crash and usage analytics)

No ad networks or data brokers are connected to Newsyze.

5. AI FEATURES & RESPONSIBLE USE

Newsyze offers AI-powered summaries, translations, flashcards, and text-to-speech. All processing is anonymized, temporary, and for educational purposes only. AI summaries are provided to support learning and understanding — they are not professional or factual advice.

6. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Data may be processed in the United States or other regions. For EU/UK/Swiss users, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards apply.

7. RETENTION

Crash and usage data are retained up to 90 days for debugging, analytics data up to 14 months, then anonymized or deleted. We do not store any personal user content or AI prompts.

8. SECURITY

We use encryption and other safeguards to protect transmitted data. However, no system is 100% secure. Please use Newsyze on secure devices and networks.

9. CHILDREN

Newsyze is designed for general audiences and not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your information. To exercise rights, email support@soruvalab.com or use our Data Deletion Form.

11. COOKIES

Our website may use minimal cookies for analytics or support forms. The mobile app itself does not use cookies or web trackers.

12. DO-NOT-TRACK

We do not currently respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals. If industry standards evolve, this Policy will be updated.

13. UNITED STATES PRIVACY RIGHTS

Certain states (e.g., California, Colorado, Virginia) provide additional rights, including data access, correction, and deletion. We have not sold or shared personal data in the past 12 months.

14. AUSTRALIA & NEW ZEALAND

We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Australia) and Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand). You may contact us or your local privacy regulator for complaints.

15. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may revise this Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.
