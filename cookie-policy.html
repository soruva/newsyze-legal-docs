Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how SoruvaLab ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use the Newsyze mobile application ("Services").

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server. They are commonly used to remember you and your preferences, either for a single visit or multiple repeat visits.

Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To ensure the proper functioning of our Services
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud
  • To gather analytics and understand usage
  • To improve performance and personalization

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for app functionality and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to analyze how users interact with the app. These include Firebase Analytics cookies.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us monitor and improve the app’s performance.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We respect users' privacy rights under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California). Users in these regions can opt out of non-essential cookies and tracking technologies via the cookie consent banner or settings page where applicable.

How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control and manage cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to block or alert you about cookies, but some parts of the Services may not function properly.

Opt-Out Options

To opt out of analytics tracking, you can submit a request via our Google Form. For more details, please visit our FAQ.
